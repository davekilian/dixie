Wikibox is a wiki for your Dropbox.

Wikis are great tools for running projects, recording ideas and collaborating with people. With a wiki you can quickly build and use your own website without the need for technical expertise.

Wikibox is different than the typical wiki. The average wiki runs a database that stores your data — with Wikibox, all your content is saved to your Dropbox. What does this mean?

  • You control your data, but you can still access your wiki from anywhere.

  • Sharing is as easy as sharing a folder in your Dropbox.

  • Developers can fork Wikibox and continue to use existing Wikibox wikis, without the headaches of data migration.
